The Gap

Women aren't behind because they can't use AI.

Women founders, investors, and operators are getting left behind. Not because they can't use AI. Because they're learning it alone. In rooms where the conversation isn't happening for them.

What Time Rich Is

A community for women who actually use AI, to run their business and their life.

Not awareness. Not dabbling. Implementation.

Application-based. Not to gatekeep, but because the quality of the room is the whole point.

Why we're here

We want you to become time rich

Because what's the use of all this AI if it doesn't buy us back a few hours to actually enjoy life? Women who step into their authority now, who build their operations on AI instead of around it, become a different kind of leader.

It's not just about learning the tools. It's about building the systems behind your work and your personal life, so you can take better care of yourself, have more fun, and deepen the relationships that matter.

It's free to join. There's an application, which sounds fancier than it is. We just care a lot about who's in the room, so culture fit matters to us.

Questions

The honest answers

What is Time Rich?

An application-based community for women founders, investors, and operators who actually build with AI. Not awareness. Not vibes. Implementation. Honestly, the fastest way to get it is to come to a workshop and feel the room for yourself.

Who is it for?

Women who are done teaching themselves AI alone in 47 browser tabs at midnight. 200+ founders, investors, and operators are already in the room, building together. Come meet a few at the next live event.

Why is it application-based?

Not to gatekeep, promise. The quality of the room is the whole point, so we keep it to people who are actually building. Culture fit matters to us. The application is lighter than it sounds.

What do I get inside?

Weekly calls, a working Slack, guides and playbooks, and direct access to 200+ women shipping AI. Plus live workshops and events where the lightbulb actually goes on.

How do I join?

Apply and tell us what you're building, we reply within 48 hours. Impatient? Skip the small talk and grab a seat at the next workshop.
