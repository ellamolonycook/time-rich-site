Questions

The honest answers

What is Time Rich?

An application-based community for women & queer founders, investors, and operators who actually build with AI. Not awareness. Not vibes. Implementation. Honestly, the fastest way to get it is to come to a workshop and feel the room for yourself.

Who is it for?

Women & queer founders who are done teaching themselves AI alone in 47 browser tabs at midnight. 200+ founders, investors, and operators are already in the room, building together. Come meet a few at the next live event.

Why is it application-based?

Not to gatekeep, promise. The quality of the room is the whole point, so we keep it to people who are actually building. Culture fit matters to us. The application is lighter than it sounds.

What do I get inside?

Weekly calls, a working Slack, guides and playbooks, and direct access to 200+ women shipping AI. Plus live workshops and events where the lightbulb actually goes on.

How do I join?

Apply and tell us what you're building, we reply within 48 hours. Impatient? Skip the small talk and grab a seat at the next workshop.

What if I want to go deeper than the free stuff?

Two ways. The AI Content Machine is my 40-day group build where you ship a system of agents that research, write and produce your content in your voice, from $197. Or go all in with 1:1 AI System Coaching — me and you, building the AI operating system for your whole business. That one's by application, only 4 spots, and I read every application myself.
