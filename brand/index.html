05 — Colour

Near-black, two greens, a cherry, a lavender

A dark operating-system palette. Void black as the ground, cool greys for structure, a green family — acid lime that signals and a deep moss for depth — cherry red for heat, and light lavender as the one soft secondary. Click any swatch to copy its hex.

Discipline: lime is for action and emphasis only — CTAs, the wordmark stop, one highlighted phrase. Moss is its darker companion — pressed states, tonal depth, and the text-safe green on light. Cherry is urgency and warnings, never body text. Light lavender #EBD7FF is the one sanctioned secondary — a soft fill for tints, quiet backgrounds and supporting accents on dark, never a CTA and never the wordmark. The retired plum/lilac brand purple stays retired; lavender is a pastel accent, not a return to it. No gradient heroes. On light grounds lime darkens to moss #3E5400 for legible text while staying bright as a fill — the tokens handle it. The values here are the source of truth.

Contrast & access

Dark ground, high-contrast text — and one rule that trips everyone up: bright lime is a fill colour, never text on white. These pairings pass WCAG AA; the last one doesn't.

14 — Motion

Motion like a terminal, not a carousel

Fast, purposeful, one moment per view. Motion should feel like a system responding — a cursor, a sweep, a reveal — never decoration bouncing for attention. Everything degrades gracefully under reduced-motion.

Thinking_ Hover the sweep → 140ms · ease-out

The cursor

The blinking lime _ is the signature — loading states, the end of a headline, a "we're on it" beat. One per screen.

The sweep

Links and CTAs get a lime underline or a → that slides on hover. 140ms, ease-out. Confirmation, not spectacle.

The reveal

Sections fade up ~12px on scroll, once. Fast and quiet. Never stagger a whole grid tile-by-tile.

✓120–200ms, ease-out. If you notice the duration, it's too slow.
✕No bounce, spin, or parallax. That's the hype content we're the opposite of.
✓One moment per view. Same discipline as one lime accent per slide.
✕Honour reduced-motion. Every state still works with animation off.
