3. How we use it, and our legal bases

  • To review applications and to run the community and The AI Content Machine course.
  • To take and manage payment for paid products (via Stripe).
  • To send you the emails you signed up for, and operational messages about your application, purchase, or the course.
  • To provide the AI-assisted features you use, by sending your prompts to our AI provider for processing.
  • To provide support and to improve and secure the Site and the Course.
  • To market our products (you can opt out of marketing at any time).
  • To comply with legal obligations and to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

Where the law requires a legal basis (for example under UK/EU GDPR), we rely on: your consent (marketing emails and testimonials); performance of a contract (delivering a course you bought and its AI features); our legitimate interests (running, improving, and securing the Site, the community, and our business, where not overridden by your rights); and legal obligation (tax, accounting, and responding to lawful requests).

4. Who we share it with (processors and partners)

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who process it on our behalf, under contract and only as needed to run the Site and the Course:

  • Stripe: payment processing and fraud prevention.
  • Cloudflare: content delivery, security, and processing/routing the forms you submit on the Site.
  • GitHub Pages: hosting the Site's static pages.
  • Notion: storing your application and contact details so we can review and respond.
  • Anthropic: processing prompt text you enter into AI-assisted features to generate a response.
  • WhatsApp / Meta: running the community group, if you choose to join it.
  • Our email and analytics providers: sending the emails you signed up for and understanding Site traffic.
  • Professional advisers, or authorities, where legally required.

Each provider has its own privacy policy and, where relevant, its own AI and data-use terms that govern their handling of your data.

6. How long we keep it

We keep your information for as long as you have a relationship with us and for as long as needed for the purposes above, then delete or anonymise it. Some records are kept longer where the law requires it: for example, we keep financial and tax records relating to payments for the minimum period required by applicable law (typically several years). Marketing contact details are kept until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete them.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent at any time (without affecting processing already carried out).

UK and EU (UK GDPR / EU GDPR): you have the rights listed above, and you may lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority (in the UK, the ICO).

California (CCPA / CPRA): you have the right to know what we collect and why, to access and delete it, to correct it, and to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information and of targeted advertising. We do not sell or share your personal information in that sense, and we do not use it for cross-context behavioural advertising. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

To exercise any right, email emc@timerich.ai. You can unsubscribe from marketing at any time via the link in any marketing email. We will verify your request against the information we hold before acting on it.

8. Cookies and analytics

The Site uses cookies and similar technologies that are strictly necessary to serve and secure the pages, and analytics to understand traffic and improve the Site. You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings; some parts of the Site may work less well if you do.

⚠ Lawyer review If you serve any non-essential analytics or marketing cookies to UK/EU visitors, you likely need a consent banner that blocks those cookies until the visitor opts in. Confirm your actual cookie set and whether a consent tool is required.

9. Children

The Site and the Course are for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has given us their data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Security and breaches

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, and we rely on the security of established providers such as Stripe and Cloudflare. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security. If a breach affects your personal data and the law requires it, we will notify you and the relevant authority within the required timeframe.

11. Changes and contact

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will change and we will flag anything material. Questions or requests: emc@timerich.ai.
