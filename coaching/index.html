Questions founders ask

The honest answers.

How is this different from the AI Content Machine?

The AI Content Machine is the group program, self-paced, from $197. This is one to one. It is custom to your whole business, not just content, and I am building it with you directly. Your coaching includes the full Content Machine program, so you get that too.

Do I need to be technical?

No. If you can describe how you work, we can build it. You approve the decisions, I handle the setup, and by the end you know how to run it. Staying independent is the whole point.

What actually ships? Am I left with something real?

Yes. Every phase ends with a working piece of your system, not notes. By the end you have a system that is running, plus a plain-language runbook so you can operate and adjust it yourself. You own all of it.

How long does it take?

It depends on how much of your business we wire up. Getting your content researched, written and produced in your voice is a shorter build, around six weeks. Wiring your inbox, admin and research into one system that runs the business goes deeper, closer to twelve. Every build is custom, so we scope the right length together on the call.

How much of my time does it take?

The build sessions, plus the odd approval between them. The point is to give you time back, not take more of it. Most of the heavy lifting happens in our sessions and in the system we are building, not on your calendar.

What if AI changes and my setup breaks?

You will own the system and know how it runs, so you can adjust it. That is why the whole thing ends in handover and a runbook. You are never dependent on me or on one tool staying exactly as it is today.

Will it actually sound like me?

Yes. It runs on your context, not a generic model. That is the difference between built on you and sounds like a robot. We spend the first week on exactly this, because everything else depends on it.

Why is it application-based?

Because I only take on a handful of founders at a time and I want the fit to be right for both of us. The application tells me your business and where your time is going. If it is not a match, I will say so and point you somewhere better.
