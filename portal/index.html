How to progress

There's a right order

Watch, then build. Then move on.

Every module is watch-then-build: we show you, you do it, you move to the next one. You'll have agents running before you finish Tier 1.

  • Start with the Brain and your Tone of Voice. Do not skip this — a machine with a rubbish brain writes rubbish content, faster.
  • Build the Research Agent, then the Caption Writer. That's a working system.
  • Stack the writers and the visual pipeline on top, in whatever order matches where your audience actually lives.

Go at your own speed, with the live calls to keep you moving — that's the entire point of 6 months of access. Some of you will binge-build it in a weekend. Some of you will do one agent a week. Both are correct.
