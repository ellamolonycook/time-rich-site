10 / How to progress

Watch, then build. Then move on.
There's a right order

Every module is watch-then-build.

We show you, you do it, you move to the next one. You'll have agents running before you finish Tier 1.

  • Start with the Brain and your Tone of Voice. Do not skip this — a machine with a rubbish brain writes rubbish content, faster.
  • Build the Research Agent, then the Caption Writer. That's a working system.

Go at your own speed, with the live calls to keep you moving — that's the whole point of 6 months of access. Some of you will binge-build it in a weekend. Some will do one agent a week. Both are correct.
